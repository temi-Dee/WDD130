About Us — Day Out Expeditions

At Day Out Expeditions, our whitewater rafting trips are designed to give thrill-seekers an unforgettable adventure on the water. Our expert guides ensure your safety while navigating through stunning landscapes and challenging rapids. Oars Up!

Our History

Founded in 1995 by a group of adventure enthusiasts, Day Out Expeditions started with a handful of rafts and a passion for exploration. What began as a small venture has grown into one of the leading providers of whitewater rafting experiences, known for safety, fun, and connection with nature.
